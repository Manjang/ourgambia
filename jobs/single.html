Job Title: Marketing Associate

Quest Digital Advertising Agency 20 Kairaba Avenue 15,000

Responsibility

The post holder is responsible for the maintenance of our website as well as producing high quality audio and video material for the MRC Unit The Gambia.

Introduction

The Communications Department of MRC Unit The Gambia (MRCG) provides supports and promotes the achievements of The Unit. Communications is a core activity of MRCG and essential. The department’s main role is to generate public awareness of MRCG’s research, share outcomes and knowledge using a range of communications products, and encourage public engagement and dialogue through variety of communication channels. To support these important objectives, qualified individuals are encouraged to apply to this vacancy.

Purpose of the Job

The post holder will be expected to maintain our website as well as complete a range of multimedia tasks to support the Communications Department and The Unit’s mission and vision.

Key Responsibilities

The post-holder will be responsible for accomplishing the following:
  • Produce audio and video content for our channels, such as the website as well as specific project requests.
  • Film and edit footage from interviews, conferences and events.
  • Write scripts.
  • Support the development of content for all of our communication channels.
  • Translate our research outputs into audio and visual content.
  • Photography and maintain our media library.
  • Supports the development of radio and TV programs.
  • Strong skills in Final Cut Pro X or another video editing software.
  • Knowledge on how to use Photoshop and Illustrator.
  • Manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Able to complete assignments on time, occasionally within tight timelines and work collaboratively with others.
